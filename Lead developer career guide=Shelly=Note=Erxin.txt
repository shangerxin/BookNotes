# What is a lead developer
- There may be times when team members disagree with your decisions, and you must be able to handle conflict and difficult conversations.

I’ve had the pleasure of working with people from various backgrounds.

Lead developers must balance technical and leadership skills, and we’ll cover both throughout this book.
